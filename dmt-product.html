DMT Cartridge

N,N-Dimethyltryptamine · 500 mg · 0.25 mL

Product Overview

The ÆTHER DMT cartridge is a 510-thread, lead-free ceramic vaporizer cartridge containing 500 mg of N,N-Dimethyltryptamine in 0.25 mL of USP-grade propylene glycol. Designed for inhalation via a compatible 510-thread battery, it is intended for intentional use in a safe and supportive environment by adults 21 years of age or older only.

Storage & Handling

Storage
  • Store upright in a cool, dark place
  • Keep between 15–25°C (59–77°F)
  • Crystallization may occur naturally
Restoring Fluidity
  • Gently warm the sealed cartridge indirectly
  • Use body heat or a warm water bath below 50°C (122°F)
  • Do not microwave
  • Do not expose to open flame

Warnings

⚠ Warning — Psychoactive Substance
  • For adults 21 years of age or older only. Keep out of reach of children and pets.
  • Do not use if pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning pregnancy. Exposure may result in serious fetal harm or developmental injury.
  • This product produces intense psychoactive effects, including altered perception, impaired judgment, confusion, anxiety, panic, loss of coordination, or psychological distress. Use may result in injury.
  • Do not drive, operate machinery, or engage in hazardous activities while under the influence.
  • The safety profile and long-term effects of this substance have not been fully characterized. Improper use or unsuitable environments may increase the risk of adverse psychological or physical effects.
  • Do not use if you have a personal or family history of psychotic disorders or severe psychiatric conditions.
  • Intended for intentional use in a safe and supportive environment.
